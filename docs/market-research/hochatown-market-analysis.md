# Hochatown/Broken Bow Short-Term Rental Market Analysis

*Research compiled: March 2026*

## Executive Summary

Hochatown, Oklahoma has become one of the hottest short-term rental markets in the country, with **2,400+ vacation rentals** serving millions of visitors annually. Most cabin investors are **out-of-state owners from Dallas-Fort Worth** who desperately need reliable local service providers. This creates a massive opportunity for The Handy Beaver.

---

## Market Size & Demographics

### Rental Inventory
- **2,400+ short-term rentals** in Hochatown/Broken Bow area (AirDNA data)
- Town officially incorporated October 2024 (now working with Granicus for STR regulation)
- Median cabin sale price: **$625,000** (up from $485,000 in 2021)
- Average annual rental revenue: **$75,000 - $160,000** depending on size/amenities

### Revenue by Cabin Size
| Bedrooms | Annual Revenue |
|----------|----------------|
| 1 BR | $35,000 - $50,000 |
| 2 BR | $55,000 - $80,000 |
| 3 BR | $75,000 - $110,000 |
| 4+ BR | $100,000 - $160,000+ |

### Investor Demographics
- **Primary origin: Dallas-Fort Worth metro** (170 miles / 2.5 hr drive)
- Secondary markets: Oklahoma City, Tulsa, Little Rock
- Profile: Second-home buyers, real estate investors, doctors/professionals
- Key insight: **Most manage remotely** and desperately need local vendors

## Investor Pain Points (THE OPPORTUNITY)

> "Don't underestimate the importance of reliable local service providers. Having great cleaning and maintenance teams makes all the difference when you're managing remotely from Texas." — Jennifer R., Hochatown cabin owner

### What Remote Owners Need:
1. **Responsive maintenance** — Can't wait 2 weeks when hot tub breaks
2. **Turnover repairs** — Quick fixes between bookings
3. **Seasonal maintenance** — Deck re-sealing, roof sweeps, gutter cleaning
4. **Emergency repairs** — Storm damage, plumbing issues
5. **Photo documentation** — Proof of work for remote owners
6. **Transparent pricing** — No surprises for out-of-state owners

### High-Demand Services for Cabins:
- Deck repair, staining, re-sealing
- Hot tub maintenance/repair
- Roof sweep and minor repairs
- Siding repair/replacement
- Trim work and touch-ups
- Railing and stairs
- Window/door replacement
- Fire pit repairs
- Outdoor lighting
- Weatherization/seasonal prep

## Seasonal Demand Patterns

| Season | Rental Demand | Maintenance Needs |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Spring (Mar-May)** | High — Spring break | Post-winter repairs, deck prep |
| **Summer (Jun-Aug)** | Very High — Families | Hot tub issues, AC, outdoor |
| **Fall (Sep-Nov)** | High — Foliage/couples | Winterization prep, roof sweep |
| **Winter (Dec-Feb)** | Moderate — Holidays | Emergency repairs, heating |

**Key insight**: Book 2-4 weeks ahead for seasonal prep work. Emergency calls have premium pricing opportunity.

## Regulatory Environment

- Hochatown incorporated with Town of Hochatown government
- STR licensing required (working with Granicus as of Oct 2024)
- McCurtain County jurisdiction
- Relatively **investor-friendly** compared to other markets
- Low-density development preserves cabin spacing and charm

---

## Investment Opportunity Zone

Broken Bow is located in a **Qualified Opportunity Zone**, making it one of the most tax-efficient markets for long-term investors. This means:
- Continued investor influx expected
- More cabins being built = more maintenance demand
- Sophisticated investors who value professional services
