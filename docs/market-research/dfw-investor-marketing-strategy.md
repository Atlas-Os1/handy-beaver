# Dallas-Fort Worth Investor Marketing Strategy

*Target audience: Out-of-state cabin investors in the DFW metro area*

## Why Target DFW?

- **170 miles** from Hochatown (2.5 hour drive)
- **Primary investor base** for Hochatown/Broken Bow cabin market
- High household incomes, investment-oriented demographic
- Pain point: Need reliable local vendors they can trust remotely
- Already active in real estate investor communities

---

## The DFW Cabin Investor Persona

### "Dallas Dan"
- **Age**: 35-55
- **Occupation**: Doctor, lawyer, tech executive, business owner
- **Income**: $150K-500K+
- **Situation**: 
  - Owns 1-3 cabins in Hochatown area
  - Manages via property manager OR self-manages remotely
  - Visits property 4-8x per year
  - Uses Airbnb/VRBO for bookings
  - Active in Facebook investor groups
  
### Key Pain Points
1. "I can't find reliable people out there"
2. "My property manager's vendors are slow and overpriced"
3. "I need someone who answers their phone"
4. "Small repairs turn into big problems when I can't be there"
5. "I need photo documentation of work done"

### What They Want
- **Responsiveness** — Answer calls/texts quickly
- **Transparency** — Clear pricing, no surprises
- **Documentation** — Before/after photos sent via text/email
- **Reliability** — Show up when you say you will
- **Local expertise** — Know the area, the materials, the climate

## Marketing Channels

#### Target Groups
- "Hochatown Cabin Owners" (if exists)
- "Broken Bow Real Estate Investors"
- "Oklahoma Vacation Rental Investors"
- "DFW Real Estate Investors"

#### Organic Strategy
- Join groups and provide helpful advice

  - Real estate investing
  - Airbnb

## Pricing Strategy for Investors

### Standard Pricing
- Under 6 hours: $175
- Full day (6+ hours): $300
- Helper rate: $100/$225

### Investor-Friendly Options

**1. Maintenance Packages**
- **Quarterly inspection**: $150 (walk-through + photo report)
- **Seasonal prep package**: $400-600 (deck, gutters, minor repairs)
- **Annual contract**: 10% discount on all services

**2. Emergency Service**
- Standard hours: No extra charge
- After-hours/weekend: +$50 trip fee
- Holiday emergency: +$100

**3. Property Manager Rates**
- 10% discount for PM referrals
- Priority scheduling for recurring PM clients
- Monthly billing option for established relationships

## Messaging Framework

### Headline Options
- "Your Trusted Local Handyman for Hochatown Cabins"
- "Cabin Maintenance for Out-of-State Owners"
- "We Take Care of Your Cabin Like It's Our Own"
- "The Maintenance Partner Your Property Manager Can't Match"

### Elevator Pitch
> "I'm a local craftsman who specializes in cabin maintenance for vacation rental owners. Most of my clients live in Dallas or OKC and need someone they can trust to handle repairs quickly and professionally. I send before/after photos of every job so you know exactly what's happening at your property."

### Objection Handling

**"I already have a property manager."**
> "Great! Many property managers use us as their preferred maintenance vendor. We often handle deck work, siding, and larger repairs that cleaning crews can't address. Happy to work with your PM directly."

**"How do I know you'll show up?"**
> "I understand the frustration. That's why I send confirmation texts before every appointment, and you'll get photos of the work in progress. I've built my business on being the reliable guy you can count on."

**"Your rates seem high."**
> "I charge a fair rate for skilled work done right the first time. My clients find it's cheaper than paying someone twice to fix the same problem. Plus, I document everything so you have records for taxes and insurance."
