## Direct Competitors

### Handyman Services (limited in area)

Based on HomeAdvisor/Angi research, there are **very few dedicated handyman services** marketing specifically to vacation rental owners.

| Found Services | Notes |
|----------------|-------|
| Generic handyman listings on Angi | Not STR-focused, poor reviews |
| Individual contractors | Word-of-mouth only, no web presence |
| Out-of-area contractors | Travel from Durant, Idabel, etc. |

**Key insight**: The market is underserved. Most "competitors" are:
1. General contractors who don't specialize in small jobs
2. Property managers doing maintenance in-house (limited capacity)
3. Unorganized word-of-mouth handymen

## Cleaning Companies

Cleaning crews are on the front lines. They see issues first.

| Approach | Value Prop |
|----------|------------|
| Find local cabin cleaning crews | Partner for referrals |
| Offer 10% referral fee for jobs | Incentivize them to call us first |
| Provide business cards to leave with owners | When they report issues to PM/owner |

**Key insight**: Cleaners find broken railings, damaged decks, hot tub issues. If we're their go-to call, we get first crack at the work.

## Competitive Advantages (vs. Alternatives)

### vs. Property Manager In-House Teams
- We're flexible — no PM overhead baked into our rates
- We handle overflow when their team is maxed
- We specialize in skilled carpentry, not just basic maintenance

### vs. Out-of-Area Contractors
- We're local — no 2-hour drive to job site
- Same-day/next-day availability
- Know the area, the materials, the climate

### vs. Word-of-Mouth Handymen
- Professional communication (answer our phone)
- Photo documentation for remote owners
- Website and reviews build trust
- Clear, upfront pricing

### vs. DIY for Remote Owners
- They're 3 hours away in Dallas
- Their time is worth more than our rates
- Small issues become big problems when ignored
