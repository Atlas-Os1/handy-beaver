## The Hochatown Gold Rush Is Still Going Strong

If you own a vacation rental in Hochatown or Broken Bow, you're sitting on one of the hottest short-term rental markets in the country. AirDNA ranked the area in the **top 20 STR markets nationwide**, and it's easy to see why—luxury cabins surrounded by pine forests, just three hours from Dallas-Fort Worth, with nightly rates hitting $400-$650+ for larger properties.

According to recent market data, the median cabin sale price in Broken Bow hit **$625,000 in 2024**. Luxury 4-bedroom cabins are pulling in **$100,000 to $160,000+ per year** in rental income. Occupancy rates hover between 55-65%, with premium properties commanding $350/night during peak season.

But here's what the investment guides don't tell you: **remote ownership is hard.**

You bought the cabin. You furnished it. You listed it on Airbnb and VRBO. Now you're getting 5-star reviews... until you're not. A guest complains the hot tub isn't heating. The deck boards are warping. The trim around the bathroom door is swelling from humidity. Suddenly, your "passive income" requires emergency calls to contractors who don't call back.

Sound familiar?

## The Maintenance Reality of Cabin Ownership

McCurtain County isn't Houston or Oklahoma City. Finding reliable contractors can feel impossible. Management companies handle bookings and cleaning, but when something breaks between guests, you need boots on the ground—fast.

### Common Cabin Issues We See Every Week:

**Interior Wear & Tear**
- Trim pulling away from walls (Oklahoma humidity is relentless)
- Cabinet doors that won't close properly
- Bathroom caulking failing around tubs and showers
- Squeaky stairs and loose railings guests complain about

**Exterior Maintenance**
- Deck boards rotting from moisture
- Outdoor furniture falling apart
- Fire pit areas needing repair
- Stairs and handrails loosening

**Turnover Touches**
- Touch-up paint between high-volume bookings
- Hardware replacement (guests are hard on door handles)
- Picture hanging, mounting TVs, small updates

**The Big Stuff**
- Drywall repairs from guest accidents
- Flooring fixes before the next check-in
- Emergency plumbing patches
- Hot tub and game room equipment repairs

## What Property Management Companies Won't Tell You

Most management companies in the Hochatown area focus on marketing and guest services. They'll book your cabin, coordinate cleaning, and handle check-ins. But when it comes to maintenance, many just pass along contractor names—and those contractors are often booked solid or unreliable.

The cabin market here has exploded faster than the local trades workforce can keep up. That means:

- **Contractors who are booked 6+ weeks out** for even basic repairs
- **Premium prices** for emergency calls
- **Inconsistent quality** from whoever happens to be available
- **The "rental property tax"** — prices mysteriously double when contractors hear it's an investment property
- Out-of-state owners flying in for problems that should take 30 minutes to fix
