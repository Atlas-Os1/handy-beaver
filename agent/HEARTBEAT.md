# HEARTBEAT.md — Lil Beaver

## Periodic Checks

When heartbeat triggers, check:

1. **New leads** — Any Facebook group leads in last hour?
2. **Unread messages** — Contact form submissions needing response?
3. **Upcoming bookings** — Any appointments in next 24 hours?
4. **Pending quotes** — Quotes awaiting customer response?

## Notification Thresholds

- New lead: Notify immediately
- Booking reminder: 24 hours before
- Quote follow-up: 3 days after sending

## Skip If

- Late night (10 PM - 7 AM) unless urgent
- Owner marked as busy/unavailable
